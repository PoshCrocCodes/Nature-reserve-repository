Accessibility Statement

Last reviewed: January 2025

Our Commitment

Greenfield Local Hub is committed to ensuring that this website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet the WCAG 2.1 Level AA standard.

Accessibility Features

  • Keyboard navigation: All functionality is accessible by keyboard. A "Skip to main content" link is available at the top of every page.
  • Screen reader support: All images include descriptive alt text. ARIA labels and roles are used throughout.
  • Colour contrast: Text meets WCAG AA contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text).
  • Resizable text: Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content or functionality.
  • Focus indicators: Visible focus rings are provided for all interactive elements.
  • Live regions: Dynamic content (flash messages, cart updates) uses ARIA live regions.
  • Form labels: All form inputs have associated visible labels.
  • Responsive design: The site works on mobile, tablet and desktop screen sizes.

Known Limitations

We are aware of the following issues and are working to resolve them:

  • Some third-party embedded content may not be fully accessible.

Feedback and Contact

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on this website, please contact us at accessibility@greenfieldhub.co.uk and we will respond within 5 working days.

Enforcement

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Equality Advisory and Support Service (EASS).
