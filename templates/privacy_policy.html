Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Who We Are

Greenfield Local Hub (GLH) is a food cooperative based in Lancashire, England. We operate as a data controller for personal data collected through this website. Our contact email is privacy@greenfieldhub.co.uk.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you create an account or place an order:

  • Name and email address
  • Delivery address and phone number
  • Order history and purchase details
  • Reviews and ratings you submit
  • Loyalty programme activity

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders (contractual necessity)
  • Manage your account and loyalty rewards (legitimate interest)
  • Send marketing emails where you have opted in (consent)
  • Comply with our legal obligations

4. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to: access your data, rectify inaccurate data, erase your data, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing. To exercise any right, contact us at the address above.

5. Data Retention

Order data is retained for 7 years to comply with UK tax law. Account data is retained until account deletion. Marketing consent records are kept for 3 years after withdrawal.

6. Cookies

We use strictly necessary session cookies for authentication and security. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. No cookie consent banner is required for strictly necessary cookies under the UK PECR.

7. Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with our hosting provider for operational purposes under a data processing agreement.

8. Complaints

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): ico.org.uk, telephone 0303 123 1113.
